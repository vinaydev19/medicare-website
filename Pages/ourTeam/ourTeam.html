How BookMyMedicare Team Works?

BookMyMedicare consists of experienced, skilled, and dedicated medical professionals led by a senior consultant to provide quality home healthcare services.

We ensure proper treatment and healthcare facilities for our patients, covering every essential aspect of home-based care.

1

Medical Assessment

Doctors assess the patient’s needs to determine appropriate care at home.

2

Technical Team Visit

The technical team visits the residence to evaluate the necessary equipment setup.

3

Equipment Installation

Medical equipment is installed and tested to ensure functionality.

4

Patient Stabilization

The patient is shifted under supervision and stabilized at home.

5

Daily Monitoring

Patient vitals are assessed virtually and physically for ongoing care and counseling.
