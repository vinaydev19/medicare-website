Palliative care at home

Palliative care aims at improving the quality of life of a patient who is suffering from a chronic serious ailment like that of advanced cancer

Things to know in palliative care?

In-home palliative care may feature the following types of interventions to support and improve the patient’s quality of life:

  1. Pain releif Medications
  2. Medical evaluations
  3. Symptom monitoring or tracking
  4. Skilled nursing, such as wound care
  5. Patient and family education
  6. Spiritual guidance if required
  7. Medical assistance for support and counselling
