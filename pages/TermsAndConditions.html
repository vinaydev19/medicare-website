Terms and Conditions

1. Introduction

Welcome to First Response Nursing Services. By accessing and using our website, you agree to comply with the following Terms and Conditions. If you do not agree with any part of these terms, please refrain from using our services.

2. Services Provided

We offer nursing services, caretaker services, pharmacy, physiotherapy, cupping therapy, acupuncture, pathology, ECG services, doctor consultations, and ambulance services. These services are available for bedridden patients, elderly care, post-operative care, and respiratory care. Additional facilities include ICU setup and medical equipment on a purchase or rental basis.

3. Service Booking

  • Users can book services directly from our website without creating an account.
  • Users must provide accurate and complete information during registration.
  • Bookings are subject to availability and confirmation.

4. Payment Terms

  • Payments for services can be made online.
  • Users agree to pay all applicable charges at the time of bookin
  • Any additional services requested may incur extra charges.

5. Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Users can cancel services at any time.
  • Refunds are available upon service cancellation, subject to our evaluation.
  • Refund requests must be submitted via our official contact channels.

6. User Responsibilities

  • Users must provide correct and up-to-date medical history and illness details.
  • Misrepresentation of medical conditions may lead to service denial.
  • Users must ensure the safety of healthcare providers during home visits.

7. Service Areas

Our services are available in Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bangalore, and Pune.

8. Third-Party Involvement

  • We work with third-party healthcare providers who may collect necessary information such as completion certificates, passport-size photos, PAN card, Aadhar card, passport, bank details, and utility bills.
